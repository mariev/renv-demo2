Return a phrase that Picard uttered

Description

Be as eloquent as our favorite archaeologist, diplomat and decorated Starfleet officer Jean-Luc Picard, or as we tend to say "JLP". The startrek package does not help you in becoming a Starfleet officer, or archaeologist for that matter. But perhaps you will use the same phrasing when you order your Tea Earl Grey. As Picard once said : "Darmok and Jalad... at Tanagra".
